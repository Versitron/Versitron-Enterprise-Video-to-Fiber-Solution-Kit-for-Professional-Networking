## What is a Video to Fiber Installation Kit?

A video to fiber kit converts analog CCTV signals into optical signals for transmission over fiber optics, enabling video transmission over distances far beyond copper limits without electromagnetic interference. These kits are ideal for system integrators, security professionals, and enterprise networks expanding beyond traditional cable constraints.
